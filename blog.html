The Impact of Unhealthy Eating

The Impact of Unhealthy Eating :

At Grow & Glow, we want you to know how unhealthy food choices can affect your health. Here are some diseases caused by poor eating habits:


1.Obesity

Eating too much junk food can lead to weight gain and increase the risk of heart disease and diabetes.

2.Type 2 Diabetes

Too much sugar in your diet can raise blood sugar levels, leading to diabetes.

3. Weak Boness

Not getting enough calcium can make bones weak and prone to fractures.

4. Heart Disease

Unhealthy fats from processed foods can block arteries and lead to heart disease.

5. Digestive Problems

Not eating enough fiber can lead to constipation and stomach discomfort.


By eating nutritious foods and staying active, you can avoid these diseases and stay healthy!
